Instructions

This set of flashcards includes the major 46 hiragana characters, which together form all the sounds of the Japanese language. Please enter what each character sounds like as lowercase letters in the answer box. Then press Enter or click Flip. If you need help, you can click the Hint button and it will display a helpful mnemonic.

Shortcuts

LEFT ARROW: Goes to front of card or previous card
RIGHT ARROW: Goes to next card (or skip)
H: Displays hint
